Why We Exist

We were founded on the belief that the great debates of history belong to everyone. By digitizing and presenting public-domain texts in clean, readable formats, we aim to give educators, students, and independent thinkers access to materials too often hidden behind academic paywalls.

From Marx and Mill to Douglass and de Beauvoir, Open Inquiry Press curates ideas that shaped our understanding of liberty, equality, and justice.

Free access to the ideas that shaped modern thought.
